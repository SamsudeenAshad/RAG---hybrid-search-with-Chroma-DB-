How it works

Your question flows through specialized agents: a planner decomposes it, a rewriter expands queries, hybrid retrieval fuses dense (Gemini) and sparse (BM25) vectors via RRF, a reranker keeps the best passages, reasoning drafts a grounded answer, and verification fact-checks it — looping back if claims are unsupported. Answers cite their sources.
